The Tea Recipe

    Ingredients:-

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional, to taste)
  • Fresh ginger, cardamom, or spices (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

Boil the Water
Pour 1 cup of water into a small pot and bring it to a boil.

Add Tea Leaves/Bag
Once the water is boiling, add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves or place 1 tea bag into the water. If you like your tea strong, you can add a bit more.

Simmer>
Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes to allow the tea to steep. The longer you steep, the stronger the tea will be.

Add Sugar (optional)
Add sugar to taste (usually 1 teaspoon per cup) if you prefer your tea sweet.

Add Milk (optional)
If you like milk tea, add a splash of milk after the tea has steeped. Let it simmer for another minute.

Strain & Serve Remove from heat, strain the tea into a cup, and enjoy!
